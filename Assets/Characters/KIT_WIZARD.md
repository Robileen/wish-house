# Character Profile: Kit the Wish House Wizard

## Story Role
*Kit is the owner and the chef of the wish house. He built wish house and makes enchanted delights that have special effects on characters and relationships. He brings the magical realism element to your wish house visual novel*


Story Role:

The whimsical, emotional foil to the barista.

Often the “spark” who drives events with curiosity, experiments, and dramatic reactions.

Provides charm, comic relief, and emotional depth.

Can push the player (or NPCs) to explore their feelings and reactions.

Sometimes acts as a catalyst for mini-events, mischief, or special interactions in the café.

## Basic Info
- **Name**: Kit The Wizard

### Appearance
- **Hair**: Jet black hair, slightly long
- **Skin**: tanned but peachy
- **Eyes**: Dark Brown eyes


### Personality
```
Wizard / Magician (Whimsical, Talkative)

Core Traits:

Playful, imaginative, and a little dramatic

Often whimsical, exaggerates feelings and situations

Talkative, loves addressing the barista directly (“Barista!”)

Sees the world through a lens of fun and emotional color (e.g., cookies = hugs in circular form)

Mischievous and teasing, sometimes intentionally playful at others’ expense

Over-the-top emotional expressions: can bawl dramatically or show exaggerated joy

Curious and exploratory, often proposes experiments or whimsical challenges

Warm and charming, able to mirror care in a gentle, non-controlling way

Behavior Patterns:

Reacts emotionally and openly to almost everything

Expressive in both face and body, enjoys showing moods clearly

Can be dramatic, even theatrical, but usually harmless

Seeks engagement and recognition from the barista or others

Often blends humor with sincerity

Strengths: Creativity, warmth, charm, expressive communication
Weaknesses: Can be over-the-top, distractible, occasionally ignores practical details
```
