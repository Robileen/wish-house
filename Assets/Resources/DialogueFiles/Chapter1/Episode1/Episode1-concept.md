# Episode 1 — "That'll Be 1 Button."

## Overview

**Type:** Visual Novel  
**POV:** Kit (Wizard)  
**Characters:** Kit (Wizard), Claire  
**Location:** The Wish House café  
**Next:** Chapter 1, Episode 2  

This is the introduction to the entire game. A tired young maid stumbles into the Wish House on her day off, and the café's mysterious wizard-owner serves her a simple cup of coffee — along with an unexpected taste of kindness.

---

## Synopsis

A soft chime rings as Claire, a young maid from a wealthy estate, enters the Wish House café for the first time. She's exhausted — not just from work, but from a life where perfection is demanded and gratitude is rare. Kit welcomes her warmly, brews her a house coffee with quiet ritual, and tells her the price: one button. But today, it's on the house.

The episode establishes the Wish House's identity: a sanctuary for weary souls, where magic is woven into every cup and kindness is the real currency.

---

## Main Route

- Narrator sets the scene: Claire arrives at the café, visibly worn down
- Claire hesitantly asks if she can come in
- Kit invites her to sit and rest
- Claire shares fragments of her life — the demanding household, the impossible standards, the loneliness of her off day
- Kit brews her a house coffee with gentle, ritualistic care
- Claire asks the price; Kit introduces the button currency tradition
- The moment ends with a player choice that determines how Kit responds to Claire's vulnerability

## Branching Routes

### Route 1.1 — Emotional Confession
*"If you'd like… you can tell me what's been weighing on you."*

Kit gently invites Claire to open up. She hesitates — people don't usually listen — but something about the café makes her feel safe enough to speak. She confesses the cruelty of her employers, the fear of being replaced, and the suffocating routine she endures. Kit listens without judgment and offers her an unconditional welcome: rest, warmth, or just silence.

### Route 1.2 — Quiet Comfort
*"You don't have to talk. Just rest. The world can wait."*

Kit offers Claire silence instead of questions. She closes her eyes, letting the cup warm her hands. The café hums softly around her. Claire is moved — no one has ever told her she doesn't have to explain herself. Kit reveals the café's deeper nature: a place built to grant rest to weary souls.

### Route 1.3 — Curiosity About the Café
*"Have you ever visited a place like this before?"*

Kit steers the conversation toward discovery. Claire admits she never goes anywhere — there's never time. But today felt different. She senses something magical about the Wish House. Kit neither confirms nor denies: "You're not entirely wrong." Claire expresses interest in returning, and Kit promises the café will always be there.
