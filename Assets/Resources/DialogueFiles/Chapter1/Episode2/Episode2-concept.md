# Episode 2 — "Help Wanted, No Experience Required?"

## Overview

**Type:** Visual Novel  
**POV:** Edward (Barista)  
**Characters:** Edward (Barista), Kit (Wizard)  
**Location:** City streets → The Wish House café  
**Next:** Chapter 1, Episode 3  

The second episode shifts perspective to Edward, a financially struggling student who stumbles upon a mysterious job posting. Driven by responsibility to his family, he makes his way to the Wish House — and meets Kit for the first time.

---

## Synopsis

School ends and Edward walks home down a cracked sidewalk, weighed down by more than his backpack. He's the eldest sibling. His mother works nonstop. His part-time job just cut his hours. Tuition and rent press in from every side.

Then a hand-drawn poster on a lamppost catches his eye: *"Help Wanted. No Experience Required."* — from a place called the Wish House. Edward has never heard of it, but desperation and hope push him to follow the address. When he arrives, Kit greets him with an uncanny perception, sensing the weight Edward carries before he even speaks. Edward explains his situation, and Kit welcomes him as the newest member of the Wish House.

---

## Main Route

- Narrator establishes Edward's world: school, family pressure, financial strain
- Edward's internal monologue reveals his sense of duty as the eldest sibling
- He discovers the Wish House job poster on a lamppost — old-fashioned, whimsical, a teacup with spiraling stars
- Edward reads it aloud: *"Help Wanted. No Experience Required."*
- He wrestles with doubt but decides to take the chance for his mother's sake
- The episode ends with a player choice that defines Edward's motivation

## Branching Routes

### Route 2.1 — Arrival at the Wish House (Duty)
*"I need the money. My family needs me."*

Edward walks to the Wish House with tired but purposeful steps. Responsibility drives every stride — his siblings count on him, his mother fights alone, and he refuses to let her. He enters the café and Kit senses a "determined aura." Edward states plainly that he needs the job. Kit recognizes his resilience and heart, and invites him to begin his story at the Wish House.

### Route 2.2 — A Curious Arrival (Hope)
*"A new opportunity… maybe this is a sign."*

Edward is drawn by curiosity and a rare spark of optimism. The poster's warm, whimsical design lingers in his mind as he walks through the old district. Kit senses "curiosity — bright, swirling, lively" when Edward enters. Edward frames his visit as exploring an opportunity, though Kit quickly reads the deeper responsibility underneath. Kit welcomes him as someone with both a noble heart and a curious one.

### Route 2.3 — A Cautious Visit (Caution)
*"Better check if it's legit first."*

Edward approaches analytically — double-checking the map, verifying the route, staying measured. Even in caution, his motivation is the same: he wants to protect his family from a wrong step. Kit greets him by sensing a "cautious aura" with "sharp eyes, logical posture." Edward explains he came to verify the posting first. Kit admires that his caution stems from responsibility rather than fear, and invites him to begin.
