🍓 Bake a Wish — Café Art Scene Ideas
1️⃣ Morning Sunlight Café
Mood: Soft morning glow, warm sunlight streaming through windows
Tables: Light wood, pastel tablecloths
Props: Coffee cups steaming, small pastries, open recipe book
Characters: M & B near counter, sunlight highlighting watercolor textures
Special Effects: Sunbeams with subtle watercolor overlay


2️⃣ Rainy Day Cozy Café
Mood: Gentle rain outside, reflective wet window panes
Tables: Darker pastel shades, small indoor plants
Props: Tea cups with steam, small puddle reflections
Characters: Customers with umbrellas outside (peek through window)
Special Effects: Soft raindrops, warm glow from inside

3️⃣ Flea Market Café Extension
Mood: Inspired by your market scene
Tables: Mix of mismatched table styles (wood, pastel, shabby chic)
Props: Clay cups from flea market, vintage teapots, small trinkets
Characters: M inspecting fruit, B arranging clay cups
Special Effects: Floating sparkles around “treasures”

4️⃣ Fruit & Citrus Theme
Mood: Bright, cheerful, energetic
Tables: Rectangular, center fruit baskets (strawberries, oranges, lemons)
Props: Plates with fruit salad, cups with juice gradients
Characters: Customers A & B seated, M & B standing at service area
Special Effects: Soft glowing hearts and floating fruit icons over satisfied customers

5️⃣ Afternoon Study Café
Mood: Calm, serene, cozy
Tables: Small library-style tables with books and tea cups
Props: Open books, pastries, little notebooks with sketches
Characters: Customers quietly reading, M & B preparing drinks
Special Effects: Warm watercolor highlights on table surfaces

6️⃣ Evening Dessert Glow
Mood: Twilight, dim café lights with pastel lamplight
Tables: Rich pastel table surfaces, candle jars or small lamps
Props: Desserts on plates, clay cups
Characters: Customers enjoying fruit salad & drinks, soft smiles
Special Effects: Glow around dessert plates, subtle sparkle on cups

✅ Consistent Art Style Guidelines
2D only, never 3D
Watercolor textures on tables, walls, props
Barbie My Scene-inspired character design
Pastel color palette (soft pinks, yellows, oranges, mint, light blues)
Overlay effects separate (glow, hearts, sparkles, food)
Modular elements (tables, props, characters)
